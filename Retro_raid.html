Retro raid

Description:

A fast-paced 2D shooting game where the player moves left and right to dodge and shoot enemies descending from above. The objective is to eliminate enemies before they pass the player. If an enemy successfully gets past, the player's health decreases, adding an element of urgency and strategy to the gameplay.

Features:

  • Four distinct enemy types, including ones that fire player-tracking bullets.
